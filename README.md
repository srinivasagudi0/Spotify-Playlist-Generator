# Spotify-Playlist-Generator

 A simple streamlit app that turns your mood into a song. It is a real work to find songs based on your feeling. Your feeling might change just trying to find a song. This app gives you recommendation based on your mood in about 10s.

 Select your mood and how many songs you want to be recommended and wait for ~10s and get your song along with link in spotify.

 Thanks for reading.
